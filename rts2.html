NAME OF SITE

Opened to the public as a State Park in 1992, the Rail Trail starts at 6,900' in elevation, near Park City, and falls, over the course of 27.7 miles, to 5,280' elevation at the eastern end. ... Closest to Park City, the trail is paved for approximately 3 miles. Thereafter, heading east, the trail is smooth, wide road-base.
