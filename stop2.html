Rails To Trails

Since 1986 the national Rails-to-Trails Conservancy has helped organize over 1,500 trail conversions. The Echo to Park City Railway was the first one in the state of Utah. The conversion of the Railway was finalized by a coordinated effort between the city, county, state, and federal officials, the Union Pacific Railroad, and A & K Railway Materials. The line was completely abandoned in 1989, and three years later, the Historic Union Pacific Rail Trail was born. In 2006 the Union Pacific Rail Trail was designated National Recreation Trail status by the Secretary of the Interior.
